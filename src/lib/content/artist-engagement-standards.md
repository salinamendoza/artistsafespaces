## Purpose

These standards describe how Artist Safespaces engages with artists across our programs: live events, public art parks, and Art Therapy festivals. They exist so artists know what to expect from us before they say yes.

## Compensation

Artists are paid for their time and their work. Day rates are set in writing before the event. We do not ask artists to work for "exposure," and we do not retain rights to original work created during our programs unless explicitly licensed in a separate agreement.

## Crediting

Every artist is credited by name on event signage, on the relevant case study page, and in any post-event press materials we control. When partners write about the program, we ask them to credit artists by name as well.

## Data and contact

We do not share artist contact information with partners or sponsors without the artist's explicit written consent. Artist information collected for an event is used for that event.

## Working conditions

On-site, artists have access to water, breaks, a private space when reasonable, and a clearly identified Artist Safespaces lead they can flag concerns to. We do not place artists in situations where they are the only point of contact for crowd management or unrelated brand activations.

## Disagreements

If something goes wrong, we want to hear it. Email standards@artistsafespaces.org. We respond to every message.
